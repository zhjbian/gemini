TSLA Q1 2026 Earnings Analysis

2. Tactical "Battleground" Levels

  • Upper Resistance: $405.20. This is the psychological barrier and the "Gap Fill" target from the early April drawdown. TSLA needs a significant beat on Automotive Gross Margins (>18%) to sustain a breakout above $400.

3. Post-ER Reaction Prediction (Historical & Trend Audit)

A. Historical "Gap" Audit (Last 4 Quarters)

TSLA exhibits a "Binary Surge/Purge" pattern. It rarely moves sideways post-earnings:
* Average Absolute Move: ~5.8%.
* The Trend Phase: Interestingly, three of the last four quarters have seen a "Mean Reversion" on Day 1 (if it gaps up significantly, it often fades; if it gaps down, it often finds a bottom mid-day).
* Extreme Outlier: April 2025 saw a +10.22% move. We are currently exactly one year from that specific cycle low.

B. Price Trend Audit (The "Coiled Spring")
  • Monthly Trend (The Rebound): TSLA has recovered significantly from its $337.24 low (April 7). The rally to $409 (April 17) was a classic "Front-Running" of AI/Robotaxi news.
  • Weekly Trend (The Cooling): In the last 4 trading days, TSLA has pulled back from $409 to $386.39 (-5.5%).
  • The Reading: This weekly dip indicates that some "Hot Money" has already exited. The stock is not overextended heading into the print, which reduces the risk of a "guaranteed" sell-the-news event.
C. Implied Volatility (IV) vs. Expected Move
  • Market Expectation: ±5% ($19–$20 move).
  • Skew: Currently slightly skewed toward Put protection ($380 floor), suggesting the market is more "afraid" of a downside miss than "greedy" for an upside beat.

Because expectations have been hammered down to $0.33 EPS (Internal Consensus) and delivery misses are already known, I predict a Bullish Bias post-ER, provided margins hold steady.

Scenario A: Bullish "Relief Rally" (65% Probability)
