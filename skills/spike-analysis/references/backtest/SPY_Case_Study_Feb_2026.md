## The Resolution (Feb 24 - March 9)
Following the Feb 17th prints, SPY entered a sharp multi-week decline.

- **Hit #1**: The 657.39 target (printed Feb 24) was hit within **9 trading days**.
- **The "Pinball" Effect**: Price dropped through the first layer of targets ($650 range) and eventually hit the **635.68** Tier 1 magnet on March 12th (18 trading days after discovery).

## Lessons for SPY Analysis
1. **Index Inertia**: Unlike TSLA, which can resolve 7% moves in 2-3 days, SPY targets often take **15-20 trading days** to resolve.
2. **Volume Tiers**: The 30+ Volume threshold in RTH/AH was the critical filter. Signals with <20 volume during this period were more prone to noise, but the **380-vol** signal was 100% accurate.
3. **Leading Indicators**: The institutional spikes provided a **4-day lead time** before the major selling acceleration began on Feb 23rd.
